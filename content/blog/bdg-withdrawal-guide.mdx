### Step-by-Step Deposit Process

**How to Deposit Funds:**

1. **Login Account**: Access your BDG Game account
2. **Go to Wallet**: Click on "Wallet" or "Add Money" section
3. **Select Amount**: Choose your desired deposit amount
4. **Choose Method**: Select your preferred payment method
5. **Enter Details**: Provide payment information (UPI ID, card details, etc.)
6. **Confirm Payment**: Verify details and confirm transaction
7. **Complete**: Wait for payment confirmation

- **Confirm**: Approve transaction in Paytm app
- **Complete**: Funds added to BDG wallet
